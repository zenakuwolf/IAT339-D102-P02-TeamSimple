About the company

The Hologame Station provides services for gamers of all ages to enhance their playing expreince by the use of augmented reality or augmented virtuality

Our goal is for gamers and those interested to play games as means of using technological advancement to enhance gaming experience. Also who wouldn't want to see duel monsters in holograms?
